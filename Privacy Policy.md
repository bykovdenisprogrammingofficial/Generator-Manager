Privacy Policy for PassWorld Manager

Last updated: May 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how PassWorld Manager ("the Application") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Application.

1. Information We Collect
The Application does not collect any personal information from you. All data generated and stored within the Application remains on your local device.

2. How We Use Your Information
The Application uses the information you provide (such as passwords and account details) solely to provide the password generation and management functionality. This information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

3. Data Storage and Security
All passwords and sensitive data stored in the Application are encrypted using industry-standard AES-256 encryption. The encryption key is derived from your master password, which is never stored on your device or transmitted elsewhere.

4. Sharing of Information
The Application does not share your information with any third parties. All data remains on your local device unless you choose to export or back it up yourself.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the Application.

6. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: passworldmanager@example.com

By using the Application, you signify your acceptance of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to this policy, please do not use the Application. Your continued use of the Application following the posting of any changes to this policy will be deemed your acceptance of those changes.
